# Tata iQ GenAI-Powered Data Analytics Virtual Experience

## 📌 Overview
This repository contains my completed work for the **Tata iQ GenAI-Powered Data Analytics Virtual Experience Program** on Forage. I worked with the Financial Services team to develop AI-driven credit risk and collections strategies.

---

## 🎯 Key Deliverables

### Task 1: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Assessed data quality from customer financial dataset (500+ records)
- Identified key risk indicators: `Missed_Payments`, `Credit_Utilization`, `Debt_to_Income_Ratio`
- Used GenAI tools (ChatGPT) to structure insights for predictive modeling

### Task 2: Predictive Modeling Framework
- Proposed a **no-code predictive modeling approach** for customer delinquency risk
- Justified **Logistic Regression** for transparency and interpretability in financial services
- Developed evaluation strategy using: Recall (target >80%), Precision (>60%), F1 Score, AUC-ROC
- Identified high-risk segments: Unemployed/Self-employed customers with low account tenure

### Task 3: AI-Driven Collections Strategy
- Designed an **autonomous collections system** using agentic AI principles
- Built 4 ethical guardrails: Fairness, Explainability, Compliance, Privacy
- Defined risk tiers: Low (<50) → No action | Medium (50-70) → Monitor | High (71-85) → Auto SMS | Critical (86-100) → Human agent
- Projected business impact: **20% delinquency reduction, 30% cost savings, $500K annual savings**

## 📊 Key Insights from Analysis

| Insight | Customer Segment | Action |
|---------|-----------------|--------|
| High credit utilization (>70%) drives risk | All customers | Proactively offer credit limit increase |
| 3+ missed payments in 6 months = high risk | Customers with poor payment history | Early intervention after 2nd missed payment |
| Debt-to-Income ratio >0.4 is a red flag | Employed & self-employed | Restructure loans or offer counseling |

## 🧠 Ethical AI Principles Applied

- **Fairness:** Monthly bias audits by employment status & location
- **Explainability:** Risk scores explained by top 3 contributing features
- **Compliance:** Full audit logs + human review for critical actions
- **Privacy:** Role-based access controls + data anonymization
